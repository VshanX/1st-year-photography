Welcome to Ocean Breeze

Experience coastal cuisine at its finest since 2005

View Menu Make Reservation

Our passion for exceptional dining experiences shines through in every dish.

Ocean Breeze combines fresh local ingredients with innovative culinary techniques to create memorable meals.

  • Daily fresh seafood delivery from sustainable sources
  • Farm-to-table produce from local organic farms
  • Expertly crafted dishes by our award-winning culinary team

Founded in 2005 by Chef Michael Laurent, Ocean Breeze has become a destination for those seeking refined coastal cuisine. Our commitment to quality and service has earned us recognition as one of the top seafood restaurants in the region. We invite you to join us for an unforgettable dining experience.

Summer Seafood Tower

A spectacular presentation of the season's freshest catches

Our signature seafood tower features chilled Maine lobster, jumbo shrimp, oysters on the half shell, Alaskan king crab legs, and ceviche. Served with traditional accompaniments including mignonette sauce, cocktail sauce, and lemon wedges. Perfect for sharing and celebrating summer's bounty.

Chef's Tasting Menu

An ever-changing culinary journey through seasonal flavors

Experience our chef's creativity with this five-course tasting menu that highlights the best ingredients of the season. Each course is thoughtfully paired with wine selections from our sommelier. The menu changes weekly based on market availability and chef inspiration. Dietary restrictions can be accommodated with advance notice.

Sommelier's Wine Pairings

Expertly selected wines to complement your meal

Our sommelier has curated special wine flights to enhance your dining experience. Choose from our "Coastal Whites" flight featuring crisp, mineral-driven wines, or the "Bold Reds" flight with robust selections that stand up to heartier dishes. Each flight includes three 3-ounce pours with tasting notes.

Garden Harvest Menu

Celebrating vegetables at the peak of their season

Our vegetarian tasting menu showcases the bounty of local farms with creative preparations that highlight each vegetable's natural flavors. Current features include heirloom tomato tartare, roasted beet carpaccio, and wild mushroom risotto. The menu changes weekly based on what's freshest at market.

Seasonal Dessert Sampler

A sweet conclusion to your meal

Indulge in miniature versions of our pastry chef's finest creations. The sampler includes our signature chocolate soufflé, seasonal fruit tart, crème brûlée, and a surprise dessert. Perfect for sharing or enjoying on your own if you can't decide on just one sweet treat.

Events

Host Your Special Occasion With Us

Birthday Celebrations

Starting at $75 per person

Make your birthday unforgettable with our customized celebration packages.

  • Private dining area for your group
  • Customized menu options
  • Complimentary birthday dessert

Our team will work with you to create a personalized dining experience that matches your vision. From intimate gatherings to large parties, we have spaces to accommodate groups of all sizes. Add-ons like floral arrangements, specialty cocktails, and photography services are available.

Private Dining

Starting at $120 per person

Exclusive spaces for corporate events, anniversaries, and special occasions.

  • Entire restaurant buyout options
  • Customized multi-course menus
  • Dedicated service staff

Our private dining rooms offer elegant settings for business dinners, family celebrations, or social gatherings. With customizable menus, audiovisual capabilities, and professional event coordination, we'll ensure your event is executed flawlessly. Capacity ranges from 12 to 120 guests across our various private spaces.

Wine Dinners

$95 per person

Exclusive pairing dinners featuring renowned wineries and special guests.

  • Five-course menu with paired wines
  • Winemaker presentations
  • Limited seating for intimate experience

Join us for our monthly wine dinner series, where we collaborate with exceptional wineries to create memorable pairing experiences. Each course is carefully crafted to complement the featured wines, with insights from winemakers or industry experts. These popular events sell out quickly - inquire about our upcoming schedule.
